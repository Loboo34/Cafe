Welcome to Savoria, a cozy cafe nestled in the heart of the city. Our cafe is a haven for coffee lovers, food enthusiasts, and anyone looking for a warm and inviting space to relax and unwind. At Savoria, we believe that food and coffee should be savored, not rushed. That's why we take great care in selecting the finest ingredients and roasts to ensure that every sip and bite is a moment of pure pleasure.

Beverages
  • Classic Breakfast : Two eggs any style, toast, and your choice of bacon or sausage.
  • Avocado Toast: Toasted sourdough bread topped with mashed avocado, cherry tomatoes, and a poached egg.
  • Blueberry Pancakes: Fluffy buttermilk pancakes with fresh blueberries, served with maple syrup.
  • Greek Yogurt Parfait: Creamy Greek yogurt layered with granola and fresh mixed berries.
  • Grilled Chicken Sandwich: Grilled chicken breast, lettuce, tomato, and avocado on a brioche bun.
  • Caprese Salad: Fresh mozzarella, sliced tomatoes, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.
  • Quinoa Bowl: Red quinoa, roasted sweet potatoes, kale, chickpeas, and tahini dressing.
  • Soup of the Day: Ask your server for today's homemade soup.
  • Espresso: A shot of strong, rich espresso.
  • Latte: Espresso with steamed milk and a small amount of foam.
  • Hot Tea: Choose from a variety of loose-leaf teas.
  • Iced Coffee: Cold brew coffee with your choice of milk and sweetener.
